Data and Website Description

This website was created to inform people on the median rent rates are for houses depending on the property's location and number of rooms within the property. Data used on this website is a subset of the full dataset found on data.gov website where the largest county or municipality was selected for each of the 50 U.S. states with the addition of the District of Columbia. Rent in this dataset includes the shelter rent plus the cost of all tenant-paid utilities, except telephones, cable or satellite television service, and internet service. Rental Facts In The U.S.

  • About 36.6% of households are renting the properties in which they reside
  • Individual investors own about 74.4% of the market share in rental properties
  • Buying is cheaper than renting in over 66% of housing markets in the U.S.
